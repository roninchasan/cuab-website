Sponsor Us!

Our team is non-competition based and research-oriented. We are trying to accomplish a task that has not been done before. A donation to this team will help us meet our goal of autonomously traversing the arts quad and further our work as engineers and intellectuals. By donating to our team, you will not only help us reach our goal, but you will also help us further our education as we test new ideas.

How to Donate

If you would like to contribute to our team's success, please follow the below instructions on how to donate.

  1. Visit the Cornell Giving Website
  2. Select "Other" under designation and enter "Cornell Autonomous Bicycle" in the "Special Instructions" area
