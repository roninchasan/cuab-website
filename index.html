Cornell Autonomous Bicycle

We are an undergraduate project team at Cornell University working to build a self-balancing, self-navigating, robotic bicycle. We are solving problems involving balance, navigation, localization, obstacle avoidance, and autonomous starting/stopping. We also work on a human-rideable bicycle with balance assist features. The students on our team study mechanical engineering, computer science, and business.
